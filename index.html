About Me

    Biologist, Data Scientist, Artist, Equity Trader, Constant Learner. Previously a graduate student and researcher. Currently building my own website and doing other programming projects. Proud to be a first-generation student and a first-generation Mexican-American.

    In my spare time, I love to cook, draw, paint, and play video games or tabletop RPGs. I've got a very competitive spirit and played Dota 2 against some of the best teams in the world around 2013. I was also Top 500 in Overwatch between 2016-2018.
